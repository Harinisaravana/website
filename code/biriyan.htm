CHICKEN BIRIYANI

1.Mix chicken, red onion, cilantro, chili powder, yogurt, tandoori paste, lemon juice, parsley, cumin
2.Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
3.Stir bread crumbs and egg into chicken mixture; form into patties. Arrange patties on a baking pan
4.Bake in the preheated oven until cooked through, 5 to 8 minutes per side.
